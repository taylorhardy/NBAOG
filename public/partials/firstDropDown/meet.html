Meet Our Pastor

Warren "Dan" Haase was born and raised in Nebraska and is one of 13 brothers and sisters who had spent their childhood years growing up in Nebraska and Iowa. He is a devout Cornhuskers fan having spent many Saturdays watching and cheering the Cornhuskers on to many victories. His deep Christian faith was nurtured by a very loving and caring mother and solidified by a stern, authoritive father who were also very faithful in their walk with Jesus and their Christian beliefs.
While still a young boy, Dan's mother had succumbed to a life threatening illness which was a major turning point in his life and sealed his decision to serve the Lord. Dan, with a deep caring love for his mom prayed to God telling Him that if He would heal his mother he would serve Him the rest of his life. God honored Dan's prayer and his mother was healed.
Dan met Jan while in Bible College, began the normal courting rituals and the two eventually married. They have two grown children, Anthony and Cheree, who have given them five beautiful grandchildren combined.
Dan and Jan lived in Broken Arrow and were members of Broken Arrow Assembly of God for over twenty years where Dan was a Royal Rangers Commander for ten of those years.
After living in California for a short while, God began to tell Dan that his place was back in Oklahoma, and after many prayers and confirmations he and Jan packed up and returned to Broken Arrow to pastor New Bethel Assembly of God.
He and Jan are very active in the church and activities and is still our awesome Pastor for which he has blessed us with his annointed, Holy Spirit led sermons since August of 2001.

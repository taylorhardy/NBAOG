U.S. and World Missions

Before being taken up into heaven, Jesus gave a wonderful challenge to His disciples called the Great Commission. It was an instruction to "go into all the world and preach the good news of Salvation to everyone, everywhere" (Mark 16:15). We continue to follow that instruction by sending and funding missionaries all over the world as they teach other cultures about the gift of salvation through Christ

Our Mission

Assemblies of God World Missions is dedicated to the fulfillment of the Great Commission through a fourfold strategy of reaching, planting, training and touching.

Reaching

We are proclaiming the message of Jesus Christ to the spiritually lost through every available means. Our early leaders, though small in number, believed Jesus' command to reach the whole world and His promise of the Holy Spirit's power to do it.

Evangelism

Penetrating the darkness with the light of the gospel has always been the leading thrust of our mission.
